<p><p>Advertising inkjet printers are categorized into three main types: roll to roll printers, flatbed printers, and other special printers. Roll to roll printers are designed for printing on continuous rolls of materials like vinyl or fabric, allowing for high-volume and continuous printing. Flatbed printers, on the other hand, have a flat surface where the materials are placed, allowing for printing on rigid materials like wood or glass. Lastly, other special printers refer to inkjet printers designed for specific applications such as printing on 3D objects or printing with specific inks or finishes.</p></p>
