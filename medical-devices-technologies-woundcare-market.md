These include foam dressings, hydrocolloids, alginates, transparent films, hydrofiber dressings, hydrogels, collagen dressings, and other specialized products. Hydrogels provide a moist wound environment and are soothing. Collagen dressings promote healing by providing a scaffold for tissue growth. Other specialized products cater to specific wound types and complexities.</p></p>
