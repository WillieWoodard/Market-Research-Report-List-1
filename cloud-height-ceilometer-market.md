<p><p>The Cloud Height Ceilometer market consists of two main types: Laser-drum Ceilometer and Optical Ceilometer. Laser-drum Ceilometers utilize a laser beam that rotates rapidly in a vertical plane to measure the distance to cloud bases, providing accurate cloud height information. On the other hand, Optical Ceilometers use infrared or laser light beams to determine cloud base and vertical visibility. Both types of Ceilometers are widely used in meteorology and aviation to monitor cloud heights, enabling accurate weather forecasting and ensuring safe air travel.</p></p>
