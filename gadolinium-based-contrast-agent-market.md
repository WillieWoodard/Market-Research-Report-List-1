Linear ionic agents have a higher risk of adverse reactions but provide better image contrast. Macrocyclic non-ionic agents offer better stability and reduced toxicity compared to linear agents. Macrocyclic ionic agents have similar properties to the macrocyclic non-ionic agents but have a positive charge, making them useful in specific imaging situations.</p></p>
