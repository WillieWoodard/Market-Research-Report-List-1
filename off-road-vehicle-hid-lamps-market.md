<p><p>Off-Road Vehicle HID Lamps are available in two market types: Built-In Ballast and External Ballast. </p><p>Built-In Ballast refers to HID lamps that have their ballast integrated within the lamp itself. This design offers convenience and portability, as the ballast does not need to be installed separately. The built-in ballast ensures efficient power supply to the lamp and simplifies the installation process.</p><p>On the other hand, External Ballast HID lamps require a separate ballast unit to be connected to the lamp for proper functioning. This design provides flexibility as it allows for different ballast options and wattages to be used, depending on specific requirements. However, it may result in a more complex installation process.</p></p>
