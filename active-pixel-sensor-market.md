<p><p>The active-pixel sensor market is comprised of various types, including buried photodiode-structure, selection transistor, and MOS amplifier. In the buried photodiode-structure, the light-sensitive photodiode is embedded within the silicon substrate, resulting in enhanced light absorption and reduced crosstalk. The selection transistor allows for individual pixel selection, ensuring improved performance and reading speed. MOS amplifier technology enables signal amplification at the pixel level, enabling better image quality and reducing noise. These advancements in active-pixel sensor technology contribute to better image capture and processing capabilities in various applications.</p></p>
