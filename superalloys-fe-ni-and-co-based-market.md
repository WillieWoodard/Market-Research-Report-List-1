Fe-based superalloys, primarily consisting of iron, exhibit excellent mechanical strength and resistance to oxidation. Ni-based superalloys, made mostly of nickel, possess exceptional heat resistance and are often used in aerospace applications. Co-based superalloys, containing cobalt as the main ingredient, are known for their outstanding resistance to wear, high temperatures, and corrosion, making them suitable for gas turbine engines and medical implants.</p></p>
