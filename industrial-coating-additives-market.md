<p><p>Industrial coating additives refer to various chemicals or substances that are added to industrial coatings to improve their performance or characteristics. There are several types of coating additives available in the market. </p><p>Rheology modifiers help control the viscosity and flow of the coating, ensuring proper application and film formation. </p><p>Defoamers are added to eliminate or reduce foam formation during coating application, preventing defects. </p><p>Dispersants aid in dispersing or stabilizing pigments or other solid particles in the coating, improving color development and stability. </p><p>Wetting agents enhance the coating's ability to spread and adhere to the substrate, ensuring uniform coverage. </p><p>Other additives include various chemicals used for specific purposes like anticorrosion, UV resistance, adhesion promotion, etc. These additives collectively contribute to improving the overall performance and quality of industrial coatings.</p></p>
