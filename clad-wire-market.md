Copper clad wire involves a copper layer over a different metal core, providing enhanced electrical conductivity and thermal performance. Precious metal clad wire includes wire with a layer of precious metals like silver or gold, utilized for applications requiring high conductivity, corrosion resistance, and superior performance. Other clad wires encompass different combinations or compositions for specific applications.</p></p>
