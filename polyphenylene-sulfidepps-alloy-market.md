PPS/PPO alloys are composed of a blend of PPS with polyphenylene oxide (PPO), providing enhanced flame retardancy and dimensional stability. PPS/PA66 alloys combine PPS with polyamide 66 (PA66), offering high heat resistance and excellent mechanical properties. PPS/LCP alloys are a combination of PPS with liquid crystal polymer (LCP), resulting in improved strength, rigidity, and dimensional stability. The "Other" category comprises various PPS alloy markets with unique compositions and attributes, providing specific properties for different applications.</p></p>
