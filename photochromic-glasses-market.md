<p><p>Photochromic glasses, also known as transition lenses, are eyeglasses that darken on exposure to sunlight and become clear again when indoors or in low light conditions. This feature makes photochromic glasses convenient as users no longer need to switch between regular glasses and sunglasses.</p><p>The global photochromic glasses market is witnessing substantial growth.
