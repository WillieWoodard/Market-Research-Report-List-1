<p><p>The screen sound technology market consists of different types of technology used to generate audio from screens. Cantilever piezoelectric ceramic sound technology utilizes a thin, flexible strip of piezoelectric ceramic that vibrates when an electric current passes through it, creating sound. On the other hand, screen exciter sound market employs small vibrators or exciters attached to the screen, which produce audio by directly vibrating the screen surface. Both technologies offer innovative ways to integrate sound into screens, providing enhanced audiovisual experiences for various applications.</p></p>
