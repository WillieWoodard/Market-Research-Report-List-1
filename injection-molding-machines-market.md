<p><p>Injection molding machines are used to manufacture plastic products by injecting molten material into a mold. This process involves injecting plastic material, usually in the form of pellets, into a heated barrel. The material is then forced under high pressure into a mold cavity where it cools and solidifies, resulting in the desired shape of the product. Injection molding is widely used in various industries such as automotive, packaging, electronics, and healthcare, among others.</p><p>The current outlook of the injection molding machines market is promising.
