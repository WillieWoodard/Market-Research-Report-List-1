<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Half Mask Respirator market consists of three main types: N-series, P-series, and R-series. N-series respirators are not resistant to oil-based particles, but they provide protection against non-oil airborne particles. P-series respirators are capable of filtering both oil and non-oil airborne particles, making them suitable for environments with oil-based hazards. R-series respirators, on the other hand, are somewhat resistant to oil-based particles but generally offer less protection compared to N and P-series respirators. Each type addresses different needs and potential airborne hazards in various industries.</p></p>
